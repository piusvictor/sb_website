Jinsi ya kumsaidia mtoto asiyeskia

SpeechBase is a 4-year (2020-2023) project aimed at supporting the development of the speech and language therapy (SLT) profession in sub-Saharan Africa. The project partners are Leibniz University Hannover, Germany; Kenyatta University, Nairobi, Kenya; Kilimanjaro Christian Medical Centre, Moshi, Tanzania; Muhimbili University of Health and Allied Sciences, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania; and University of Pretoria, South Africa. One main aim of the project is the development of the SpeechBase website, which will contain various information and education materials in the context of SLT, aimed at different target groups
